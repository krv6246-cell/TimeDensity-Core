TIME-RESULT CONTRACT

Pay for Results.
Not Hours.

The TRC model redefines employment by treating time as a second currency. When you finish in 4 hours what took 8 — the 4 saved are yours.

ODI
Output Density Index
Measures the ratio of actual output to contracted time window. The core KPI of time efficiency.
CRR
Contract Result Rate
Tracks the percentage of contracts completed within the agreed time-result parameters.
TRR
Time Reward Rate
The ratio of time returned to the worker as a reward for early high-quality completion.
