Stop Checks

Short pauses to restore clarity, rhythm, and the density of time.

Stop Check: Breath
Three calm breaths. Feel your body returning to the present moment.
Stop Check: Posture
Align your spine. Drop your shoulders. Keep your neck free. Your body is a vertical line of attention.
Stop Check: Focus
What is most important now? One action. One step. One focus.
Stop Check: Rhythm
Check your pace. Are you speeding up or slowing down? Return to your natural rhythm.
Stop Check: Presence
Feel the space around you: light, sound, body. You are here.
